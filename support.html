Frequently Asked Questions

How does the credit system work?

Each user starts with free credits. Each action (generating, optimizing, or tailoring a CV, or creating a cover letter) uses 1 credit. You can purchase additional credits as needed.

Can I edit my CV after generation?

Yes! After your CV is generated, you can edit all sections including personal information, experience, education, skills, and more.

What languages are supported?

CV Manager AI supports 7 languages: English, Spanish, French, Arabic, Dutch, German, and Russian. You can generate CVs in any of these languages.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Your data is stored securely and we never share your personal information with third parties.

Can I download my CV as PDF?

Absolutely! After creating or editing your CV, you can download it as a professional PDF document ready for job applications.

What is ATS-friendly?

ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly means your CV is formatted in a way that automated systems used by employers can easily read and parse your information.
