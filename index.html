Resume Success & Career Guides

Expert advice to help you master the modern job search.

1. Mastering ATS in 2025

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) have become more sophisticated. To pass, your resume must use standard headings, clean formatting, and relevant keywords found in the job description. Our AI-powered builder handles this automatically.

2. The Power of Action Verbs

Instead of saying "Responsible for," use strong action verbs like "Spearheaded," "Engineered," or "Optimized." This shows direct impact. CV Manager AI suggests the best professional verbs based on your specific industry.

3. Quantifying Your Achievements

Numbers tell a story. Saying you "Increased sales by 25%" is much more powerful than saying you "improved sales." Our AI tools help you identify exactly where you can add metrics and percentages to stand out.

4. Multi-Language CV Best Practices

When applying for global roles, a localized CV is essential. Whether you need a French CV, a German Lebenslauf, or an Arabic resume, understanding cultural nuances is key. Our platform supports 7+ languages.
